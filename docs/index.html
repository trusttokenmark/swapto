About TrustToken & TrueUSD

TrustToken is creating a more efficient and inclusive global financial system by digitizing assets that can be seamlessly and securely exchanged across markets worldwide. TrustToken’s first product is TrueUSD [TUSD], a stablecoin redeemable 1-for-1 with U.S. dollars and the industry’s pioneer in using third-party trust companies and independent accounting firms. TrustToken recently launched three additional fiat-backed stablecoins, TrueGBP, TrueAUD and TrueCAD. TrustToken products are trading on some of the world’s top exchanges - Binance, Huobi, OKEx, and many others - and can be purchased or redeemed directly in the TrustToken App. For more information, visit trusttoken.com.
